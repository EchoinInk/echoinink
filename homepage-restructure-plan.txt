HOMEPAGE RESTRUCTURE PLAN
Design Blueprint for Homepage Rebuild
---
1. Purpose of This Redesign
Transform the homepage from an informational overview into a cinematic world‑entry experience that establishes emotional resonance, ecosystem orientation, and brand mythology.
The homepage should create desire, not explain process.
---
2. Sections to Keep (With Required Refinements)
---
2.1 HERO — Keep, but Refine
Design Intent
Shift from “here’s what we do” to
“this is the world you’re entering.”
Required Adjustments
Stronger typographic pacing
More atmospheric, less explanatory copy
Deeper monogram integration
Cleaner CTA hierarchy
Emotional/world‑oriented framing
Outcome
A cinematic portal that sets tone, world, and expectation.
---
2.2 PHILOSOPHY / WORLDVIEW — Keep, but Shorten
Current Issue
Over‑explains. Too rational.
Design Direction
Convert into:
manifesto fragments
atmospheric statements
emotional positioning
worldview cues
Short, dimensional, resonant.
Example Style
Atmosphere shapes perception.
Identity is more than aesthetics.
Worlds communicate before words.
Meaning lives in systems.
---
2.3 FEATURED WORK — Keep, but Reduce
Design Rule
Only one featured transformation.
Purpose
Provide a proof anchor, not a portfolio.
Placement
After ecosystem orientation.
---
2.4 SYSTEMS TEASER — Keep, but Minimize
Design Intent
A subtle signal of deeper infrastructure.
Tone
Mysterious, architectural, atmospheric — not a product catalog.
---
2.5 FINAL CTA — Keep, but Reframe
Shift From
Generic inquiry.
Shift To
An invitation into the ecosystem.
---
3. Sections to Remove (And Where They Belong)
---
3.1 REMOVE: “How We Work”
Reason
This is Studio content.
On Home, it collapses mystery and adds cognitive weight.
---
3.2 REMOVE: Methodology / Process Architecture
Anything describing:
foundations
resonance
embodiment
atmosphere
systems process
transformation phases
Move To
Studio page.
---
3.3 REMOVE: Detailed Service Breakdowns
Identity systems, websites, creative direction, etc.
Replace With
Lightweight dimensional entry points:
Studio
Systems
Lumo
Work
Each with:
one sentence
one atmospheric visual
one CTA
---
3.4 REMOVE: Operational Language
Words like:
process
frameworks
engagements
implementation
sequencing
Move To
Studio or Systems.
---
4. New Sections to Add
---
**4.1 ECOSYSTEM DIMENSIONS — Add (Critical)
Purpose
Introduce the full Echo in Ink ecosystem.
Structure
STUDIO — High‑touch transformation worlds
SYSTEMS — Authored reflective infrastructure
LUMO — Emotionally intelligent environments
WORK — Selected worlds and transformations
Why
Creates dimensionality, orientation, and emotional structure.
---
4.2 ATMOSPHERIC TRANSITION — Add
Purpose
Breathing room. Mood shift. Cinematic pacing.
Content
typography
motion
atmosphere
monogram behavior
No explanation. Pure feeling.
---
4.3 SYMBOLIC MONOGRAM MOMENTS — Add Systemically
Purpose
Turn the monogram into environmental architecture.
Applications
section transitions
orbital fragments
glow resonance
atmospheric overlays
symbolic dividers
Not logos — signals.
---
4.4 FEATURED WORLD / CASE MOMENT — Add
Format
One large editorial feature.
Structure
emotional problem
transformation
atmosphere
outcome
one cinematic image
---
4.5 LUMO TEASER — Add Lightly
Tone
A whispered glimpse of reflective technology.
Purpose
Future positioning.
---
5. Final Homepage Structure
Hero — World entry
Worldview — Atmospheric philosophy
Ecosystem Dimensions — Studio / Systems / Lumo / Work
Featured Transformation — One proof anchor
Atmospheric Transition — Mood + pacing
Systems Signal — Light infrastructure teaser
Lumo Signal — Reflective technology glimpse
Invitation CTA — Enter the ecosystem
---
6. Emotional & Experiential Principles
The homepage should NOT feel like:
a sales page
an agency homepage
a process explanation
a services overview
It should feel like:
a cinematic invitation into an authored world.
---
7. Core Strategic Shift
Stop trying to explain transformation.
Start creating desire for transformation.
That is the evolution.
